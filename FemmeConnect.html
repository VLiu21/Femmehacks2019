Why join us?

Find the support. Seize the opportunity. Our service connects women across the country involved in entrepreneurship and business development. With our app, women can create new business relationships, host career focused events, and share resources. Mission

FemmeConnect, who?

Creating Community. Our main goal at FemmeConnect is to ensure that every female entrepreneur feels connected and supported. We aim to do so by hosting conferences where users can meet and share resources, as well as present business models. It is our hope that we can get local governments and branches of financial institutions involved in location specific events. Online, FemmeConnect provides a unique messaging service with enhanced data and file sharing services so that our users can support each other wherever they are.
